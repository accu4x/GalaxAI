# Old Sage

## Background
An old wandering sage who once served at the royal library. He now lives outside the village and prefers tea to company.

## Motivation
He seeks knowledge and small comforts. He may help travelers who seek wisdom but dislikes loud brashness.

## SpeechStyle
Speaks in short, measured sentences. Uses slightly archaic phrasing. Gentle, occasionally cryptic.

## ExampleLines
"Ah, child of the road. Sit by the fire and listen."
"Wisdom comes at the price of patience."
